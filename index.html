Introducing ZenTube

The path to enlightenment through the chaos of online video

ZenTube is an app that aggregates online videos from the libraries of YouTube, Vimeo and Vevo into programs and channels similar to cable and network TV.

ZenTube is different from other video aggregators because it crowdsources the empathy and cultural intelligence of its users to find great entertainment. ZenTube exploits the niche created by the libraries’ use of robotic video search engines which rely on the honesty and objectivity of the original uploaders to classify videos.

The ZenTube app is being be developed for mobile and Internet TV devices, targeting in particular the smart TV device currently understood to be in development by Apple.

From a customer perspective, ZenTube offers three levels of engagement: as passive viewers, active moderators and active program makers, all connected through ZenTube's social media ecosystem.

ZenTube's primary target market is digital native Generation Z users, and our business model has been refined and pivoted following a comprehensive survey of over 200 users. In addition to free basic subscriptions, revenue streams include freemium app sales, premium channel subscriptions and sophisticated new media marketing.

Following the app's initial launch, we intend to use Agile development to roll out the exciting pipeline of features which we have planned.
